anteater

- anteaters are south american mammals that eat ants and termites
- they grow to between 1.8m and 2.2m long
- their toungue can be up to 60cm long

alpine marmot

- large ground squirrels native to the alps and other mountainous areas in europe
- it can weigh between 1.9kg and 8kg
- they eat grasses and herbs and hold their food in their forepaws while eating
- lives at heights of 800m to 3200m

capybara

- capybaras are large rodents native to south america
- they are the largest species of rodents
- it can weigh between 35kg and 66kg
- they are semi-aquatic

red-necked wallaby

- red-necked wallabies are medium marsupials similar to kangaroos
- they are native to southern australia and tasmania
- they can weigh between 13.8kg to 18.6kg
